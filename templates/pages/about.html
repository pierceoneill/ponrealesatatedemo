We Search For The Perfect Home

Residential Sales, New homes, Letting’s and Valuations.

PON Real Estate provides a professional estate agency service for our clients with interests in property throughout Kildare & the greater Dublin area. Established in 1995, the company’s experience of selling, letting and managing property is beyond any other. With that being said, We don’t just offer renting and sales services we also have extensive experience in delivering a high standard of property valuation services for our clients.

Our team of real estate professionals is dedicated in providing a superior & professional service based on many years experience in the industry. We are a passionate team of property professionals who are experts in our field areas and focused on delivering the best results for our clients.

If you have questions in relation to your property, or if you are interested in one of our properties advertised on our website we would be glad to give you our expert advice in relation to your queries.
